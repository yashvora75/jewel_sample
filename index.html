The Shine Shiply Story

About Us

A Decade of Brilliance

Founded in 2014, Shine Shiply was born from a singular vision — to create jewellery that transcends trends and becomes a part of your legacy. What began as a small atelier in Mumbai has grown into one of India's most respected luxury jewellery houses.

Every piece in our collection is handcrafted by master artisans who bring decades of expertise to their craft. We source only the finest ethically mined gemstones and precious metals, ensuring that each creation meets our uncompromising standards.

Our design philosophy bridges the timeless aesthetics of Indian heritage with contemporary sensibility — resulting in pieces that are as meaningful as they are beautiful. We don't just create jewellery; we craft heirlooms that tell your story for generations.
